Mexican Tinga

Home


Description

Tinga is a Mexican dish of tender shredded chicken in a spicy tomato and chipotle chile sauce served on crunchy tostada shells. This recipe is easy to make and great for feeding a crowd!


Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Canned goods
  • Chicken
  • Chicken
  • Tostada shells
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the onion.
  2. Purée the tomatoes and chipotle peppers in a blender.
  3. Add the purée to the onions on the stove, stir in the chicken, and simmer.
  4. Dollop the chicken mixture onto tostadas and top with sour cream.
